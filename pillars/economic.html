E2

Economic

Buying Black Blocks to counter gentrification. Building Black Blocks through Web3, blockchain, and cooperative ownership models that build generational wealth.

Building Black Wealth, Block by Block

Economic sovereignty is not a slogan — it is a survival strategy. When a community controls its own economic infrastructure, it controls its own destiny. When it doesn't, it is subject to the decisions of others: landlords, developers, corporations, and politicians who may not share its interests.

E5 Enclave's Economic pillar is built on two mandates: Buy Black Blocks — acquire and hold land and property in Black neighborhoods before gentrification displaces residents. Build Black Blocks — create the businesses, cooperatives, and financial structures that keep wealth circulating within the community.

"Ownership is not optional. It is the difference between building a future and renting someone else's."

Core Initiatives

Buy Black Blocks

Strategic land and property acquisition in historically Black neighborhoods. Counteracting displacement and gentrification by securing community-controlled real estate before speculative capital arrives.

Build Black Blocks

Web3-powered cooperative ownership models, blockchain-based property records, and community investment vehicles that democratize wealth creation and protect collective assets.

Micro-Enterprise Incubation

Seed funding, mentorship, and operational support for Black-owned businesses. From food trucks to tech startups, we invest in local entrepreneurs who reinvest in their communities.

Financial Literacy Programs

Workshops on budgeting, investing, credit repair, homeownership, and business finance. Building the financial knowledge base that generational wealth requires.

Web3 for Community Wealth

Blockchain technology is not a gimmick — it is an infrastructure for trust, transparency, and community governance. E5 Enclave uses Web3 tools to create economic systems that are owned by the community, not controlled by distant institutions.

From tokenized community investment vehicles to transparent supply chain tracking for FarmBlock produce, our approach uses technology to solve real problems: accountability, access, and equitable distribution of value.

50+ Green Jobs Goal
$2M Community Value Target
100% Community Controlled

Every dollar that circulates within the community before leaving it multiplies its impact. Our economic programs are designed to maximize that circulation — creating local jobs, supporting local businesses, and building assets that stay in the neighborhood.
