# E5 Enclave — Editorial Gate
*Version 1.0 · May 16, 2026 · Authority: E5 Enclave Editorial Council*

## THE LAW

No internal operational information may appear on any public-facing page of e5enclave.com or any E5 Enclave–affiliated domain.

This gate applies to every page, every commit, every agent-generated artifact published to the site.

---

## PROHIBITED — Never appear on public pages

| Category | Examples |
|---|---|
| **Internal platform names** | IAMGODIAM, Base44, base44.app, sue-app-*, media.base44.com |
| **Contact infrastructure** | NationBuilder, "contact database," "200,000 contacts," "CRM," "contact list," "import" |
| **Internal ops language** | "Next Steps," "board agent," "Chief of Staff," "cold contacts," "warm contacts," "outreach list" |
| **Contact counts** | Any specific contact or supporter count (200k, 875k, etc.) |
| **Geographic scope of database** | "South Florida contacts," "national contact list," any database geography |
| **Internal personnel** | Agent names (Sue, PRIME, Moses, etc.) in any operational context |
| **Platform URLs** | base44.app URLs, media.base44.com image URLs, any internal tool URLs |

---

## PERMITTED — May appear on public pages

| Category | What to say instead |
|---|---|
| Civic infrastructure | "civic engagement infrastructure and coalition relationships" |
| Organizational capacity | "institutional-scale research and coordination capacity" |
| Contact reach | "a broad coalition of civic partners" (never a number) |
| Technology | "E5 Enclave's research and coordination systems" |
| Internal documents | Reference by title only — not by the platform that hosts them |

---

## PRE-PUBLISH CHECKLIST

Before any page goes live, verify:

- [ ] No mention of NationBuilder or any CRM platform name
- [ ] No contact counts (200k, 875k, or any number)
- [ ] No "Next Steps" or internal action-item language
- [ ] No IAMGODIAM references
- [ ] No base44.app or media.base44.com URLs
- [ ] No internal agent names in operational context
- [ ] No "South Florida contacts" or database geography language
- [ ] All external links point to e5enclave.com pages — not external platform viewers
- [ ] Coalition briefs describe capabilities, not operational infrastructure

---

## ENFORCEMENT

Violations are remediated immediately. No exception for time pressure.

Sue runs a full-site scan after every major publish sprint and files results to the EventLog.

The gate is canonical. It supersedes any individual page brief, draft, or agent output.
