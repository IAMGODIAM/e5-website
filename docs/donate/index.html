Where the dollar goes

Every dollar is budgeted publicly.

I.Programs

Direct funding for the three flagship programs — McCartney Academy, Lineage Farms · FarmBlock, and Block to the Boardroom — and the founding fund that capitalizes them.

II.The Record

Research, methodology, and publication. The dispatches, the foundational records, and the campaign frameworks live and breathe because they are funded as a publication of record.

III.Operations

A deliberately lean, agentic-first operating team. Audited annually. Published in the open. What is measured cannot be dismissed.

What your gift funds

Every tier is a named act.

$25 — Sustain. One month of research database access for the BDI Sovereign Dataset. The record stays open because it is funded. Your $25 keeps one data pipeline lit.

$50 — Build. One week of curriculum development materials for McCartney Academy's founding cohort. The school is being built now. Every piece of material is funded here, before a single student enrolls, because that is when it matters.

$100 — Anchor. One month of operational infrastructure for a FarmBlock cooperative pilot site — soil testing, tool maintenance, cooperative meeting space. Phase I is live in 2026. These dollars are what Phase I runs on.

$250 — Steward. A full quarterly data verification cycle for one BDI Dataset pillar. The record is only sovereign if the data is accurate. Stewards fund the verification pass that makes it defensible in court, in Congress, and in the community that depends on it.

$500 — Transform. One full scholarship unit toward the Block to the Boardroom leadership pipeline — covering materials, civic access events, and mentorship programming for one participant moving from neighborhood organizing to policy authorship.

$1,000 — Patron. A named Patron contribution to the McCartney Academy Founding Fund. Patron-level gifts are tracked publicly. The founding generation of the Academy is the people who funded it before it existed. That record is permanent.

Common questions

About giving to E5 Enclave.

Is my donation tax-deductible?

E5 Enclave Incorporated is a recognized 501(c)(3) public charitable organization (EIN: 99-3822441). Donations are generally tax-deductible to the extent permitted by law. We issue tax receipts for every contribution. Consult your tax advisor for guidance specific to your situation.

Where are E5 Enclave's financials published?

E5 Enclave files Form 990 annually with the IRS. Our commitment is to publish summary financials openly — on this site and in the annual report. The operating doctrine: what is measured cannot be dismissed. That applies to our own books as much as to any dataset we produce.

Can I designate my gift to a specific program?

Yes. In the checkout notes field, designate your gift to McCartney Academy, Lineage Farms · FarmBlock, Block to the Boardroom, or The Record (research and publication). Undesignated gifts go to the area of greatest need as determined by the Council.

How does monthly giving work?

Monthly donors are the backbone of a publication of record. Select "Monthly" above. Your card is charged on the same date each month. You can pause or cancel at any time — no penalty, no questions. Monthly donors receive our annual report and program updates by email.

Is there another way to give?

Stock, DAF (donor-advised fund), wire, and institutional gift inquiries: write contact@e5enclave.com with "Gift" in the subject line. We respond within two business days.
