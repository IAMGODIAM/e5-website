— Frequently Asked Questions —

Common questions.

What E5 is, how we work, how the Coalition operates, how to support, and how to apply.

## What is E5 Enclave? E5 Enclave Incorporated is a 501(c)(3) Lineage-based Think Tank — a publication of record, a coordinating institution, and a Coalition of the Willing. We convene Black-led organizations, individual builders, partner institutions, and aligned coalitions to repair, rise, and govern the lineage's future. Founded Juneteenth, MMXXVI, in Liberty City, Florida. EIN: 99-3822441. ## What is FarmBlock? [FarmBlock](/programs/lineage-farms/) is the blockchain-anchored governance layer of our flagship Environmental program, *Lineage Farms*. It pairs cooperative urban agriculture with a transparent, member-governed ledger — every contribution recorded, every distribution rule member-authored, every audit interface open to the membership. ## What is Restitution 246? [Restitution 246](/restitution-246/) is the Enclave's lead campaign under the Emancipated pillar — a disciplined wage-loss and opportunity-loss framework for the 246 years of stolen labor under American chattel slavery. Anchored to the Craemer (2015) wage-hour methodology and the Lloyd Kelly framework. R246 is *one disciplined lane* of the larger reparations question; it does not claim to settle the whole. ## How does the Coalition work? The Coalition follows a **33/17/50** model: 33% institutional members (Black-led organizations), 17% individual builders, and 50% reserved seats for partner institutions and aligned coalitions. Member organizations remain *sovereign* — we coordinate, we do not absorb. [Read more about the Coalition →](/coalition/) ## How can I support the work? - **Donate** to the founding fund: [/donate/](/donate/) - **Apply** to the Coalition: [/coalition/apply/](/coalition/apply/) - **Buy** through the store: [/store/](/store/) (proceeds support programs) - **Partner** as an institution or coalition: [/contact/](/contact/) - **Cite** our records in your work — and keep us honest ## Are donations tax-deductible? E5 Enclave Incorporated is a 501(c)(3) public charitable organization (EIN: 99-3822441). Donations made through our official channels are generally tax-deductible to the extent permitted by law. Please consult your tax advisor for guidance specific to your situation. We provide donation receipts for tax purposes. ## How do I join a program? Each [program page](/coalition/) names its own application path. The fastest single point of entry is the [Coalition application](/coalition/apply/) — note in the message which program(s) you're interested in (McCartney Academy, Lineage Farms · FarmBlock, Block to the Boardroom). ## How do I get on the press list? For interview requests, citations, and substantive coverage, write [press@e5enclave.com](mailto:press@e5enclave.com). The full [press kit](/press/) lives at /press/. ## How do organizations partner with E5? Black-led organizations apply to the Coalition through the [application form](/coalition/apply/). Partner institutions, foundations, and aligned coalitions reach out via [contact@e5enclave.com](mailto:contact@e5enclave.com) — we route to the right desk and respond within five business days. ## Where can I read the founding documents? The three founding documents — *Parallel Negros*, *Measure the Wound*, and *Restitution 246* — are published on the Enclave's reading surface and indexed in [The Record](/record/). --- ## Still have a question? Send a message via [our contact page](/contact/), or write directly to [contact@e5enclave.com](mailto:contact@e5enclave.com). We respond within five business days.
