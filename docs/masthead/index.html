Masthead

Editorial direction, programs, campaigns, and the elders we honor.

The contributors below are the standing masthead of E5 Enclave's founding edition. Roles are descriptive, not exhaustive — every member of the Coalition contributes time, talent, and treasure beyond what the masthead names.

Editorial

Israel Lee — Chairman & Founder. Author of Parallel Negros, Measure the Wound. Convener of the Coalition. Liberty City, Florida.

The Enclave — Editorial direction. The institutional voice; the standing register against which every record is audited.

Hermie — Visionary intelligence layer. The agentic-first staff that produces institutional-scale output from a deliberately lean human team.

Programs

McCartney Academy — Educational pillar. Lineage-centered K–12 institution, in foundation phase. Named in honor of Ralph McCartney.

Lineage Farms · FarmBlock — Environmental pillar. Cooperative urban agriculture coupled with blockchain-anchored governance.

Block to the Boardroom — Engagement pillar. The pipeline from neighborhood organizing to policy authorship to board service.

Campaigns

Restitution 246 — Lloyd Kelly Framework, Craemer (2015) methodology. Published in The Record · № III.

The Five Questions — Doctrinal anchor. Each Restitution 246 claim answers all five before publication.

In Memoriam

Ralph McCartney — Community elder, Liberty City. Honored on the floor of the United States Congress for decades of service. The Educational pillar's flagship academy bears his name.

The Lineage — Past, present, and future. The dead, the living, and those not yet born. The work is theirs.

The Coalition — Of the Willing. Those who walk with us — named, contributing, accountable.
