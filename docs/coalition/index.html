How the Coalition works

Sovereign organizations, coordinated work.

I.33 / 17 / 50

Coalition seats follow a 33/17/50 model: 33% institutional members (Black-led organizations), 17% individual builders, 50% reserved seats for partner institutions and aligned coalitions. The model is published, audited, and rebalanced annually.

II.Sovereign capacity

Member organizations remain sovereign. The Enclave coordinates; it does not absorb. Member data, leadership, and futures stay with their organizations.

III.Open record

Coalition membership is published openly. Conflicts of interest are declared. The list above is the founding masthead — the Coalition expands by application.
