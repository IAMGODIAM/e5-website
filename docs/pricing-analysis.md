## 5. Key Insights & Recommendations

### Highest Margin Products (Net %)
1. 🥇 BDI eBook Digital — 93.5% (pure profit minus fees)
2. 🥈 Dragon Art Print Pack Digital — 92.8%
3. 🥉 Poster 18x24 — 60.6%
4. Black Dragon Tee — 57.9%
5. Ceramic Mug 11oz — 55.2%

### Highest Margin Products (Net $)
1. 🥇 Premium Hoodie — $32.47/unit
2. 🥈 Black Dragon Tee — $20.28/unit
3. 🥉 Poster 18x24 — $15.15/unit
4. Dad Hat — $12.86/unit
5. BDI eBook — $12.14/unit

### Strategic Recommendations

1. **Push digital products aggressively** — 93%+ margins with zero fulfillment risk
2. **Tee is the volume hero** — best combo of margin ($20.28) and purchase likelihood
3. **Hoodie is the revenue hero** — $32.47/unit, promote as flagship premium item
4. **Bundle strategy:** Tee + Hat bundle at $60 (saves customer $7, you still net ~$26)
5. **Stickers are gateway products** — low margin but drive brand awareness and repeat purchases
6. **Free shipping threshold at $75** encourages hoodie + accessory combos
7. **Dad Hat margin is tight at 40.2%** — consider raising to $35 (44.9% net) if market allows
8. **Phone Case at 42.6% is acceptable** — competitive market keeps price anchored at $25

### Break-Even Analysis (Fixed Costs Not Included)

All products are profitable from unit 1 due to print-on-demand model. No inventory risk.

Monthly fixed costs to cover:
- Snipcart subscription: ~$0 (included in transaction fees)
- Domain/hosting: ~$20/month
- Webhook serverless functions: ~$5/month
- Marketing budget: variable

**Break-even at ~$25/month fixed costs = 2 tee sales or 1 hoodie sale**
