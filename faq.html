Frequently Asked Questions

FAQ

What is E5 Enclave Incorporated?

E5 Enclave Incorporated is a Miami-based 501(c)(3) nonprofit dedicated to Black empowerment through environmental sustainability, economic self-reliance, educational advancement, and equality.

What is FarmBlock?

FarmBlock is our flagship initiative integrating urban agriculture with Web3 and community-centered governance—turning vacant lots into productive, life-giving spaces and building lasting local capacity.

How can I support the mission?

You can support E5 Enclave by donating through our Donate page, volunteering your time, or partnering with us on programs and initiatives.

Are donations tax-deductible?

If you donate through the official E5 Enclave channels, your donation may be tax-deductible as allowed by law. Please consult your tax advisor for guidance.

How do I volunteer?

Visit the Volunteer page to learn about upcoming opportunities and how to get involved with community programs.

Can organizations partner with E5 Enclave?

Yes. We welcome collaborations with community groups, educators, operators, and mission-aligned partners. Use the Contact section of the site to start the conversation.

Still have a question?

Send us a message from the Contact section, or start with volunteering or donating.
