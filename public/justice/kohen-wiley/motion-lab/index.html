02 · A child before a case

His life is the first fact.

“The record begins with Kohen—not with power, the officer, or the debate.”

Kohen was one year old. Police gunfire killed him after an officer fired into a vehicle outside the Walmart in Senatobia, Mississippi. The campaign challenges unequal access to footage: agencies may see proof while a grieving family waits. His family, neighbors, and supporters continue to call for the relevant video and a full public record. The public may read and share every identified source free.
