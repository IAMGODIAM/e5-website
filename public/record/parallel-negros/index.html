The Record

Parallel institutions,
parallel histories.

Before integration, the lineage built parallel institutions — schools, banks, insurers, hospitals — that worked. Parallel Negros documents what was built, what it produced, and what dismantling it cost.

What was built

Black Wall Streets, mutual-aid societies, independent schools and presses. Self-sufficient infrastructure the lineage owned and ran.

What it teaches E5

E5 Enclave is not inventing something new — we are rebuilding what worked, with the tools of our era. The parallel institutions are the blueprint.
