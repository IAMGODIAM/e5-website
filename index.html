Who We Are

Building Power. Building Institutions. Building Legacy.

E5 Enclave Incorporated is a Miami-based 501(c)(3) nonprofit dedicated to Black empowerment through environmental sustainability, economic self-reliance, educational advancement, and equality.

Rooted in Liberty City and honoring the legacy of community builders like Ralph McCartney — an Overtown activist who rebuilt schools and secured critical infrastructure for Black neighborhoods — E5 Enclave builds the institutions and power structures that secure freedom and prosperity for Black communities.

We operate with a volunteer workforce, leveraging innovative green infrastructure, Web3 tools, and community-centered design to create durable, self-sustaining programs that outlast any single grant cycle.

Our Vision

A world where Black communities thrive through ownership, innovation, and cultural preservation — counteracting gentrification, promoting economic renaissance, and empowering the next generation of leaders.

Flagship Initiative

FarmBlock

Where urban agriculture meets Web3 innovation.

FarmBlock transforms vacant urban lots into smart, sustainable food production sites using IoT sensors, automated FarmBot technology, and blockchain-powered supply chain transparency. Our pilot sites in Pine Bluff, Arkansas and Liberty City, Miami demonstrate that technology and community can work together to build food sovereignty.
