<p class="first">The Coalition opens applications for its inaugural cohort: an incubator for lineage-based organizations prepared to convert their ideas into institutional capacity.</p>

The incubator transforms aligned organizations into a position of strength from which their ideas can actualize in the world for the lineage. The substance is concrete. Most 501(c)(3) organizations operate without ever claiming the resources for which they are already approved — the **Google Ad Grant**, **Microsoft nonprofit licensing**, **OpenAI for Business at the nonprofit rate**, **Anthropic at the nonprofit rate**. The Enclave provides the liaison work that closes that gap, and the strategic counsel that compounds it.

## What the cohort receives

- **Institutional infrastructure** — the agentic-first operating model, calibrated to the cohort organization's pillar.
- **Resource activation** — the unclaimed nonprofit-tier tools the cohort organization is already approved for.
- **Strategic counsel** — quarterly convenings with the Enclave Council, peer cohort members, and named advisors.
- **Coalition membership** — full voting standing for the duration of the cohort and an open invitation to renew.
- **A seat at The Record** — opportunity to publish a foundational record under the Coalition's editorial discipline.

## Who is eligible

Eligibility opens with established 501(c)(3) status. **It does not close there.** Organizations without 501(c)(3) status but with strong ideas and the right motivation are encouraged to apply; the Coalition supports formation as part of its mandate.

## How to apply

Applications open on **Juneteenth · MMXXVI** and run through the Eden Launch period.
