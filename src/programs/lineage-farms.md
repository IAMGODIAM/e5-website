<p class="first">Lineage Farms · FarmBlock is the flagship program under the Environmental pillar — and the most architecturally ambitious of the Enclave's founding programs. It pairs *cooperative urban agriculture* (the ancient practice) with a *blockchain-anchored governance layer* (the new tool). Land is community-held. Labor is community-recorded. Distribution is community-governed. *Every contribution counts. Every distribution is recorded. The ledger does not forget.*</p>

## What it is

**Lineage Farms** is the cooperative agricultural enterprise — community-held parcels in Miami-Dade County, regenerative practices, member-governed crop and distribution decisions, and a distribution network calibrated to undo food apartheid in the neighborhoods we work from.

**FarmBlock** is the governance layer that makes the cooperative durable: a blockchain-anchored ledger that records member contributions of labor, capital, and stewardship; smart-contract distribution rules that the membership itself authors and amends; and an audit interface that any member can open and read. *Radical transparency, on rails.*

## Why the chain

The Black cooperative tradition is centuries old and the model works — when it works. The historical record is also full of cooperatives that fell apart not from a flawed economic model but from the slow erosion of trust: opaque accounting, concentrated authority, the absence of an audit surface members could rely on. FarmBlock is not a substitute for human governance. It is *governance infrastructure* — a record that does not depend on any one administrator's recollection.

## Pillar alignment

Environmental pillar. Adjacencies: Economic (cooperative ownership and circular economics), Engagement (the civic-literacy work that flows out of cooperative governance), Educational (FarmBlock as a teaching surface for cooperative economics, agriculture, and applied chain technology).

## Roadmap

- **Phase I (2026):** founding parcels secured; member-charter ratified; FarmBlock ledger v1 deployed; first growing season under cooperative governance.
- **Phase II (2027):** distribution network expanded; second and third parcels brought online; FarmBlock ledger v2 with audit interface for members.
- **Phase III (2028+):** replication kit published — any aligned cooperative can adopt the FarmBlock pattern under a permissive license.

## Doctrine

*The first remedy any community owes itself is the capacity to feed itself, on land it stewards, by labor it values.* Lineage Farms · FarmBlock is the flagship because it is the most concrete embodiment of that doctrine the Enclave has built.
